Samurai Jack

About Jack

A Japanese samurai, who, after nearly defeating the ultimate evil being known as Aku, is sent forward in time to a dystopian future ruled by the tyrannical shape-shifting demon.

Jack's Characteristics

  1. He is a master archer, marksman, martial artist, melee fighter, and swordsman.
  2. He is multilingual due to his travels around the world in his native era, Jack was taught by many different cultures, including African, Greek, Russian, Mongolian, English, and more.
  3. He has enhanced longevity (retains youthful appearance) due to an unintended side effect of Aku's time-traveling spell.
